ABOUT MIA

My name is Mia, and I am a Multimedia Designer student. I am a creative and committed - soon to be - 30 year old girl, with great passion for graphic design and web development. My skills is a mixture of what I have learned at KEA and self-taught

I started with photoshop and illustrator several years ago, and through the Multimedia education I have gotten even more experience with the Adobe programs, such as InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop and Premiere Pro.

I have a flair for the creative, and a great interest in constantly improving my skills, both as a designer and programmer.

I am a stabile HTML og CSS coder, and I am constantly evolving as a programmer. At this current time, I have chosen Frontend Design as my elective to further enhance my skills in JS and SVG.

Furthermore I have an interest in media production and photography, and I have gained a wide knowledge of video production through past courses.

I have worked with cutting in Premiere Pro, planning, Adapted Screenplay theory and as a videographer in several school projects, one of which was nominated for a award at Cinematekets Filmfestival 2017.

I have previously been headhunted for freelance jobs through social media, which has given me experience with graphic projects for clients.

Personally I am a very outgoing and free minded girl, who possesses a lot of humor and will to evolve both as a person and professionally. I work well in teams, as well as independently. I function both as a “worker bee” and as a managing element on projects. I am very dutiful and a perfectionist, who takes great pride in delivering a complete product.

I am also a giant nerd and gamer. I was born with a computer in my hands and has since a very young age been fascinated by the fantasy and sci fi universe. Before I started as a multimedia designer I have worked as a professional trainer, judge, rider and breeder of horses.
